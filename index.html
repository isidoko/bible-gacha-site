Bible Gacha

A Christian gacha game where practicing daily habits of Bible study and prayer rewards you with in-game currency you can then use to pull Bible characters, heroes of the faith, and partnered creators from the gacha system.

FAQ

Q: Will the Bible be in the game?

A: Bible translations besides KJV require permission to use due to copyrights. We may be able to get permissions for some Bible versions, but for now this project will be a tracker app and not a Bible app until we get permission to use those translations.

Q: What audiences will this game target?

A: 15-30 year old Christians of both genders. This app will be safe for children to use. The best time to develop life long habits is during childhood, and the whole point of the game is to help believers develop healthy habits.

Q: Will AI be used for the game?

A: No parts of the finished game will contain AI, whether art, game dev, writing, etc.
